Medical-Director risk-based review. PHI-free — no names/DOB. Each encounter gets a 1–5 risk score: 5 = mandatory review (adverse event, suspended infusion, override), 4 = high-risk ingredient or missing gate, 3 = comorbidity/doc gap. The monthly sample pulls 100% of score-5 charts + the highest-risk remaining to reach 5. Each encounter carries an ID; the clinic maps the ID to its own chart to verify GFE / consent / monitoring.

Audit purpose only. AllyOS records de-identified encounter metadata (date, protocol, compliance gates, risk signals, overrides, adverse events, signing provider, outcome) to support Medical-Director oversight; it holds no PHI — patient identity stays in the clinic's own records, linked by Encounter ID.
