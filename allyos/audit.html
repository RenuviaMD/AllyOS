Medical-Director review of the practice — de-identified encounters. PHI-free: no patient names or DOB. Each encounter carries an ID; the clinic maps the ID to its own chart.
Audit purpose only. AllyOS records de-identified encounter metadata (date, protocol, compliance gates, overrides, adverse events, signing provider, outcome) to support Medical-Director oversight. It holds no PHI; patient identity stays in the clinic's own records, linked by Encounter ID. Advisory decision-support — the treating provider and the clinic's Medical Director own every clinical decision and signature. © RenuviaMD® PLLC.
