Medical-Director risk-based review. PHI-free — no names/DOB. The tool ranks encounters by clinical risk (per the screening reference: IV NAD+, high-dose Vit C, Mg+Ca together, glutathione+asthma, comorbidities, overrides, adverse events) so you pull the highest-risk charts. Each encounter carries an ID; the clinic maps the ID to its own chart to verify GFE / consent / monitoring.

Audit purpose only. AllyOS records de-identified encounter metadata (date, protocol, compliance gates, risk signals, overrides, adverse events, signing provider, outcome) to support Medical-Director oversight; it holds no PHI — patient identity stays in the clinic's own records, linked by Encounter ID.
