Provider worklist for the Good Faith Exam (FORM-INTAKE-GFE-01). Performed & signed by MD / DO / NP / PA — independent of the nursing station. Clearing a GFE here feeds the patient's card and the read-only report popup at chairside. Adults ≥ 18 · wellness scope only.
GFE is a provider clinical-judgment act (MD/DO/NP/PA) — an RN cannot perform or sign it. This module is the provider's workspace; the nursing station shows status only and opens the finished report read-only.
