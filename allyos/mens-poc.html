Wellness optimization decision support. Wellness goals qualify a man for evaluation; testosterone is considered only after provider review of symptoms, repeat AM androgen labs (total + free/bioavailable T vs your lab ranges), fertility goals, and the safety gates. Dosing appears only when the review reaches a candidate decision with gates clear — shown as a starting regimen the provider titrates to mid-normal and signs (testosterone is a Schedule III controlled substance; prescribe under your own DEA / state authority). Not a prescription; the treating provider owns and signs every decision. PHI stays on this device.
