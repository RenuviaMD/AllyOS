# Men’s Testosterone / Wellness HRT Decision-Support Module v0.1

Date updated: 2026-06-30

Scope: testosterone-based men’s HRT and men’s wellness triage. This is a safety-gate and candidate-pathway module, not a dosing protocol.

## Core design
Men’s wellness goals such as energy, vitality, libido, performance, recovery, and body composition can trigger evaluation. They do not automatically trigger TRT.

TRT candidate logic requires symptoms/signs consistent with testosterone deficiency plus unequivocally and consistently low morning testosterone with repeat confirmation.

## Modules
- Intake classifier
- Testosterone deficiency diagnostic gate
- Wellness non-testosterone pathway
- Primary vs secondary hypogonadism workup
- Fertility preservation gate
- Prostate/PSA/BPH gate
- Hematocrit/polycythemia gate
- CV/BP gate
- OSA/LUTS gate
- Monitoring and response module

## Hard stops / review gates
- Fertility desired near term
- Breast/prostate cancer or concerning PSA/prostate findings without evaluation
- Elevated or missing hematocrit
- Untreated severe OSA
- Severe LUTS/BPH symptoms
- Uncontrolled heart failure
- MI/stroke within 6 months
- Thrombophilia
- Uncontrolled BP or unstable CV symptoms
- Testosterone requested only for energy/vitality/cognition/weight loss without diagnostic criteria
