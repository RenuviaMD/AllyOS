Elamipretide (SS-31)

Mitochondrial-dysfunction/fatigue and healthspan support
FDA StatusInvestigational
Evidence GradeGrade B−
ClassificationPhysician-review
LiabilityPrescriber assumes full risk

Mechanism

Cardiolipin-binding mitochondrial peptide — stabilizes inner membrane, improves ATP/electron transport

Clinical notes

Investigational; trials in mitochondrial myopathy mixed. Trial dose 40 mg SC; compounded longevity use is lower and empiric. Watch injection-site reactions.

Indication

Mitochondrial-dysfunction/fatigue and healthspan support. <!-- NOTE: investigational; trial dose 40 mg SC daily, compounded use is lower/empiric -->

Rule out (contraindications)

  • Active malignancy (precautionary — absolute)
  • Pregnancy
  • Lactation
  • Age <18
  • Hypersensitivity

Rx

  • Dose: 5–10 mg SC daily (empiric); trial dosing 40 mg SC daily
  • Route: SC
  • Reconstitution: 10 mg + 1 mL BAC water = 10 mg/mL = 10,000 mcg/mL → 10 mg = 1.0 mL = 100 units (U-100)
  • Cycle: 4–8 weeks
  • Stack pairs: mots-c, epitalon

Labs (baseline)

  • CMP, CBC, lipids
  • Baseline fatigue/functional metric; cancer screen current

Patient education key points

  • Experimental; human results so far are mixed.
  • Injection-site reactions are the most common issue.
  • We track energy/function and stop if no benefit.
  • Report unusual or persistent symptoms.

Evidence

21 registered trials, but the **pivotal Phase 3 in mitochondrial myopathy (MMPOWER-3) missed endpoints and was terminated**; AMD/Barth ongoing; a Ph2a aging trial recruiting (NCT07275424). No approved indication.
