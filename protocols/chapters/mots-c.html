MOTS-c

Metabolic/healthspan support — insulin sensitivity and metabolic flexibility
FDA StatusResearch-only
Evidence GradeGrade D→C
ClassificationResearch-only / exclusion
LiabilityPrescriber assumes full risk
FDA PCAC — 2026-07-23. Under PCAC review for the 503A compounding list — non-binding, not FDA approval.

Mechanism

Mitochondrial-derived peptide — activates AMPK, improves metabolic flexibility (exercise mimetic)

Clinical notes

No human therapeutic trials — preclinical only. Tie use to a number (HOMA-IR/A1c) and stop if it doesn't move. Exercise is the proven version of this mechanism.

Indication

Metabolic/healthspan support targeting insulin sensitivity and metabolic flexibility. <!-- NOTE: no human therapeutic RCTs; preclinical only -->

Rule out (contraindications)

  • Active malignancy (precautionary — absolute)
  • Pregnancy
  • Lactation
  • Age <18
  • Hypersensitivity

Rx

  • Dose: 5–10 mg SC, 2–3×/week
  • Route: SC
  • Reconstitution: 10 mg + 1 mL BAC water = 10 mg/mL = 10,000 mcg/mL → 5 mg = 0.50 mL = 50 units (U-100)
  • Cycle: 4–8 weeks tied to a metabolic endpoint
  • Stack pairs: ss-31, epitalon, tesamorelin

Labs (baseline)

  • Fasting glucose/insulin (HOMA-IR), A1c ← gating endpoint
  • Lipids, CMP, CBC; cancer screen current

Safety & compliance gate

  • Investigational consent signed (`consent_investigational_peptide`)

  • Cancer screen current ← gating

Patient education key points

  • Experimental; no human efficacy proof.
  • Exercise and diet do the proven work here.
  • We'll measure your metabolic markers and stop if they don't improve.
  • Tell us any cancer history; report unusual symptoms.

Evidence

Preclinical-dominant; human data observational/associational. First interventional **Phase 2a RCT now recruiting** (NCT07505745, prediabetes/obesity, n=120). No completed human efficacy trials.
