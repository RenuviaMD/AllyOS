# Peptide Stack → IV Ingredient Axis Match Matrix v0.1

**Date updated:** 2026-06-29  
**Purpose:** match individual IV/IM ingredients to peptide stacks, peptide axes, and wellness goals.

## Correct wellness logic

This matrix does **not** require deficiency to match an ingredient.

A client wellness goal can trigger ingredient matching:

- energy
- better skin
- better hair/nails
- glow
- recovery
- performance
- calm/focus
- libido support
- body composition
- mitochondrial support

Deficiency, low intake, symptoms, or abnormal labs are **confidence modifiers**, not mandatory requirements.

## Critical separation

- **Ingredient matching:** “This ingredient aligns with the client’s wellness goal and peptide axis.”
- **Safety gate:** “Do not infuse if red flags/hard stops are present.”
- **Evidence label:** “How strong is the evidence for the expected wellness result?”
- **Prohibited claim:** “This ingredient makes the peptide work better.”

## Evidence labels

| Label | Meaning |
|---|---|
| W2 | Biologic/nutrient wellness support; stronger when deficiency/risk exists. |
| W3 | Mechanistic axis-aligned support; human outcome evidence limited. |
| W4 | Market/common wellness support with weak direct outcome evidence. |
| A | Strong deficiency/clinical-use evidence, but deficiency is not required for wellness matching. |
| S | Safety gate only. |

## Example

GHK-Cu + client wants hair/skin:

- Match: biotin, vitamin C, glutathione, B-complex/B12, trace-mineral logic if selected.
- Evidence language: skin/hair/nail wellness support; strongest biology for vitamin C/collagen and GHK-Cu skin remodeling; biotin is allowed for wellness aesthetic support but nondeficient outcome evidence is limited.
- Safety gate: biotin lab-interference warning.

MOTS-c + SS-31 + client wants energy:

- Match: Niagen/NR or NAD+, B-complex/B12, magnesium if safe, glutathione if redox goal.
- Evidence language: mitochondrial energy-pathway support; no guaranteed energy result.
- Safety gate: NAD/NR cardiovascular/high-risk review; magnesium renal gate.
