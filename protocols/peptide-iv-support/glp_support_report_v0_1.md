# GLP Support IV/IM Stack v0.1

**Date updated:** 2026-06-29  
**Scope:** wellness support for GLP/GIP/amylin pathway clients.  
**Not scope:** disease treatment, GLP medication adjustment, insulin adjustment, weight-loss guarantee, dose/rate/admixture instructions, or regulatory logic.

## Active peptide axis

This module applies to:

- Semaglutide / GLP‑1 receptor agonist axis
- Tirzepatide / GIP‑GLP‑1 axis
- Retatrutide / GIP‑GLP‑1‑glucagon axis
- Cagrilintide / amylin analog axis
- Pramlintide / amylin analog axis

## Wellness goals that can trigger support

Deficiency is **not required**. A wellness goal can trigger support, but evidence language must remain honest.

- Energy
- Hydration
- Low intake support
- Body-composition support
- Metabolic support
- Hair/skin/nail aesthetic support
- Recovery / lean-support
- Travel/heat exposure support
- Constipation with low fluid intake

## Core IV/IM options

1. GLP Hydration / Electrolyte IV  
2. GLP Energy IV or IM B‑Complex/B12 Support  
3. GLP Metabolic / MIC / L‑Carnitine Support  
4. GLP Hair / Skin / Nail Beauty Support  
5. GLP Recovery / Amino Support IV  
6. GLP IM Support: B12 / MIC / L‑Carnitine Shots  

## Safety gates first

The AI must check hard stops before recommending support:

- Severe/persistent vomiting or diarrhea
- Orthostasis, syncope, inability to keep fluids down
- CKD/AKI risk
- Severe abdominal pain, jaundice, pancreatitis/gallbladder red flags
- Insulin/sulfonylurea with missed meals or hypoglycemia symptoms
- CHF/fluid overload/ESRD/advanced CKD before fluid IV
- Magnesium renal gate
- Biotin lab-interference gate
- Vitamin C high-dose/G6PD/stone/CKD gate
- Pregnancy/lactation review

## Client-facing safe language

“This is GLP wellness support for hydration, energy, nutrient status, beauty, recovery, or metabolic-support goals. It is not presented as making GLP medication work better or increasing weight loss.”
