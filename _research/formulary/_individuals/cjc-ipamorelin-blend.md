# CJC-1295 + Ipamorelin (blend)

## Indication
Combined GH-axis support for recovery, body composition, and sleep in symptomatic adults with low-normal IGF-1.

## Rule out (contraindications)
- ☐ Active malignancy (absolute)
- ☐ Untreated severe hypothyroidism
- ☐ Pregnancy
- ☐ Lactation; age <18
- ☐ Hypersensitivity

## Rx
- **Dose:** ~100 mcg CJC + 200–300 mcg ipamorelin SC nightly
- **Route:** SC, bedtime
- **Reconstitution:** 5 mg CJC + 5 mg ipa co-vialed + 2.5 mL BAC = 2 mg/mL each = 2,000 mcg/mL → 0.15 mL = **15 units** (~300 mcg each)
- **Cycle:** 12–16 weeks
- **Stack pairs:** tesamorelin, aod-9604

## Labs (baseline)
- **IGF-1, TSH/free T4 ← gating**
- Fasting glucose, CMP, lipids

## Ongoing care
| When | What to check | What to adjust |
|---|---|---|
| Wk 2 | Tolerance, fluid retention | Reinforce timing |
| Mo 1 | Symptoms, glucose | Hold/adjust |
| Mo 3 | IGF-1, glucose | Titrate to upper-normal IGF-1 |
| Mo 6 | IGF-1, composition | Lowest effective dose |

## Patient education key points
- Not FDA-approved; compounded combination.
- One bedtime injection, empty stomach.
- Report swelling, joint pain, tingling.
- We monitor IGF-1 and blood sugar.
