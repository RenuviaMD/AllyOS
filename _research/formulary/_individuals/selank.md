# Selank

## Indication
Anxiety/stress reduction and cognitive support without sedation. <!-- NOTE: not FDA-approved; Russian-origin; native Selank SC has a short half-life (intranasal is conventional) — SC dosing here is empiric; N-acetyl/amidated analogs are more stable for SC -->

## Rule out (contraindications)
- ☐ Pregnancy
- ☐ Lactation
- ☐ Age <18
- ☐ Hypersensitivity
- ☐ Untreated primary anxiety/mood disorder needing formal care

## Rx
- **Dose:** 300 mcg SC daily; range 250–500 mcg (divided if higher)
- **Route:** SC
- **Reconstitution:** 10 mg + 2 mL BAC water = 5 mg/mL = 5,000 mcg/mL → 300 mcg = 0.06 mL = **6 units** (U-100)
- **Cycle:** 2–4 weeks, pulsed
- **Stack pairs:** semax, dsip

## Labs (baseline)
- Generally none required
- Anxiety/mood screen

## Ongoing care
| When | What to check | What to adjust |
|---|---|---|
| Wk 2 | Tolerance, anxiety | Continue |
| Mo 1 | Mood/stress effect | Hold/adjust |
| Mo 3 | Off-cycle status | Plan next pulse |
| Mo 6 | Durable benefit | Continue vs stop |

## Safety & compliance gate
- ☐ Investigational consent signed (`consent_investigational_peptide`)
- ☐ Patient education delivered (`patiented_selank`)
- ☐ Source pharmacy verified
- ☐ Mood/anxiety screen documented

## Patient education key points
- Not FDA-approved; not a treatment for diagnosed anxiety disorders.
- Now given as a small subcutaneous injection, non-sedating — rotate sites.
- Report worsening mood or unusual symptoms.
- Seek formal care if anxiety is significant.
