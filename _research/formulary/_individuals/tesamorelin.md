# Tesamorelin (Egrifta)

## Indication
Reduction of excess visceral adipose tissue — on-label in HIV-associated lipodystrophy; off-label for VAT reduction. <!-- NOTE: brand Egrifta substantially more expensive than compounded; pricing reflects compounded tier -->

## Rule out (contraindications)
- ☐ Active malignancy (absolute)
- ☐ Untreated severe hypothyroidism
- ☐ Pregnancy / disrupted HPA axis
- ☐ Lactation; age <18
- ☐ Hypersensitivity to tesamorelin/mannitol

## Rx
- **Dose:** 2 mg SC daily
- **Route:** SC (abdomen), rotate
- **Reconstitution:** 2 mg vial + 2.1 mL sterile water; inject 2 mL ≈ 2 mg. <!-- NOTE: per Egrifta SV labeling -->
- **Cycle:** 12–26 weeks; reassess VAT
- **Stack pairs:** cjc-ipamorelin-blend, aod-9604, mots-c

## Labs (baseline)
- **IGF-1, TSH/free T4 ← gating**
- Fasting glucose/A1c, lipids, CMP

## Ongoing care
| When | What to check | What to adjust |
|---|---|---|
| Wk 2 | Tolerance, site | Continue |
| Mo 1 | Glucose, symptoms | Hold/adjust |
| Mo 3 | IGF-1, A1c, waist | Titrate; keep IGF-1 in range |
| Mo 6 | VAT/waist, A1c | Continue if responding |

## Patient education key points
- FDA-approved for HIV-related belly fat; other uses are off-label.
- Daily injection; may affect blood sugar — we monitor.
- Report swelling, joint pain, or tingling.
- Tell us any cancer history.
