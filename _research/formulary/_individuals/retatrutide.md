# Retatrutide

## Indication
Investigational use for severe obesity/metabolic disease not at goal on approved incretins. <!-- NOTE: not FDA-approved; Phase 3 pending — fully investigational -->

## Rule out (contraindications)
- ☐ Personal/family MTC or MEN-2 (absolute)
- ☐ Acute pancreatitis
- ☐ Severe gastroparesis
- ☐ Pregnancy / planning; lactation; age <18
- ☐ Hypersensitivity
- ☐ Uncontrolled tachyarrhythmia (glucagon-driven HR rise)

## Rx
- **Dose:** 2 mg SC weekly start; titrate slowly toward 8–12 mg <!-- NOTE: trial titration; compounded dosing empiric -->
- **Route:** SC weekly
- **Reconstitution:** 12 mg + 1.2 mL BAC water = 10 mg/mL → 2 mg = 0.20 mL = **20 units** (U-100)
- **Cycle:** Chronic (investigational)
- **Stack pairs:** mots-c

## Labs (baseline)
- A1c, fasting glucose, CMP, lipids, **resting HR/ECG if cardiac risk**
- Pregnancy test; **MTC/pancreatitis screen ← gating**

## Ongoing care
| When | What to check | What to adjust |
|---|---|---|
| Wk 2 | GI tolerance, HR | Slow titration |
| Mo 1 | Weight, BP, HR | Advance if tolerated |
| Mo 3 | A1c, weight %, HR | Titrate to goal |
| Mo 6 | A1c, lipids, HR, weight | Maintenance dose |

## Safety & compliance gate
- ☐ Investigational consent signed (`consent_investigational_peptide`)
- ☐ Patient education delivered (`patiented_retatrutide`)
- ☐ Source pharmacy verified
- ☐ MTC/pancreatitis screen + baseline HR ← gating

## Patient education key points
- Experimental — not FDA-approved; long-term safety unknown.
- May raise heart rate; report palpitations.
- Same GI and pancreatitis cautions as other incretins.
- Report severe abdominal pain immediately.
