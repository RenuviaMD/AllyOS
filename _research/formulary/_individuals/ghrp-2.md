# GHRP-2 (Pralmorelin)

## Indication
No US therapeutic approval. Diagnostic GH-stimulation use abroad; reported off-label for GH elevation. Reference entry only.

## Rule out (contraindications)
- ☐ Active malignancy
- ☐ Hypersensitivity
- ☐ Caution: appetite/cortisol/prolactin effects

## Rx
- **Dose:** *reported* (marketplace): ~100-300 mcg SC 1-3x/day, often stacked with a GHRH analog.
- **Route:** SC
- **Cycle:** no validated protocol

## Labs (baseline)
- IGF-1, TSH/free T4, fasting glucose/A1c

## Safety & compliance gate
- ☐ Contraindications excluded; honest evidence-grade (D) disclosed
- ☐ Source pharmacy verified
- ☐ Enhanced/experimental informed consent where investigational

## Patient education key points
- Evidence grade D; status reviewed and signed by the treating physician.
- Report unexpected symptoms; source purity may be unregulated for non-approved peptides.
