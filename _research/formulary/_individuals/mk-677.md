# MK-677 (Ibutamoren)

## Indication
No approved indication. Reported oral use for GH/IGF-1 elevation, body composition, and sleep. Reference entry, not a recommendation.

## Rule out (contraindications)
- ☐ Active or prior malignancy
- ☐ Heart failure / fluid-retention risk
- ☐ Uncontrolled diabetes / insulin resistance
- ☐ Pregnancy / lactation
- ☐ Hypersensitivity

## Rx
- **Dose:** *reported* (marketplace): 10-25 mg orally at night. No validated regimen.
- **Route:** oral
- **Cycle:** reported cycling; no validated protocol

## Labs (baseline)
- IGF-1, fasting glucose/A1c, CMP, lipids; weight/edema; cardiac history

## Safety & compliance gate
- ☐ Contraindications excluded; honest evidence-grade (C/D) disclosed
- ☐ Source pharmacy verified
- ☐ Enhanced/experimental informed consent where investigational

## Patient education key points
- Evidence grade C/D; status reviewed and signed by the treating physician.
- Report unexpected symptoms; source purity may be unregulated for non-approved peptides.
