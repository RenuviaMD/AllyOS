# Cerebrolysin

## Indication
Cognitive support in post-stroke, TBI, or neurodegenerative decline. <!-- NOTE: EU/RU/AR-labeled; not FDA-approved -->

## Rule out (contraindications)
- ☐ Pregnancy
- ☐ Lactation
- ☐ Age <18
- ☐ Hypersensitivity
- ☐ Severe renal impairment
- ☐ Status epilepticus / uncontrolled epilepsy

## Rx
- **Dose:** 5–10 mL daily ×10–20 day course
- **Route:** IM (≤5 mL) or slow IV (larger volumes)
- **Reconstitution:** Pre-mixed ampoule (215.2 mg/mL solution) — **no reconstitution**
- **Cycle:** 10–20 day course, repeat 2–4×/year
- **Stack pairs:** semax, selank, dsip

## Labs (baseline)
- CMP (renal), CBC
- Seizure/neuro history review

## Ongoing care
| When | What to check | What to adjust |
|---|---|---|
| Wk 2 | Tolerance, cognition | Complete course |
| Mo 1 | Cognitive/functional change | Reassess |
| Mo 3 | Off-cycle status | Plan next course |
| Mo 6 | Durable benefit | Continue vs stop |

## Safety & compliance gate
- ☐ Off-label/investigational consent signed (`consent_investigational_peptide`)
- ☐ Patient education delivered (`patiented_cerebrolysin`)
- ☐ Source pharmacy verified (counterfeit risk)

## Patient education key points
- Not FDA-approved in the US; used as IV/IM courses.
- Porcine-derived — tell us about allergies.
- Report any seizure activity or dizziness.
- Source authenticity matters — we verify supply.
