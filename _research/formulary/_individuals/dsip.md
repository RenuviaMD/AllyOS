# Delta sleep-inducing peptide (DSIP)

## Indication
Sleep-onset/quality support as an adjunct in stress-related insomnia. <!-- NOTE: human evidence inconsistent -->

## Rule out (contraindications)
- ☐ Pregnancy
- ☐ Lactation
- ☐ Age <18
- ☐ Hypersensitivity
- ☐ Untreated OSA / primary sleep disorder

## Rx
- **Dose:** 100–300 mcg SC at bedtime
- **Route:** SC
- **Reconstitution:** 5 mg + 2 mL BAC water = 2.5 mg/mL = 2,500 mcg/mL → 200 mcg = 0.08 mL = **8 units** (U-100)
- **Cycle:** 4–6 weeks
- **Stack pairs:** selank, cerebrolysin

## Labs (baseline)
- Generally none required
- Sleep history / OSA screen

## Ongoing care
| When | What to check | What to adjust |
|---|---|---|
| Wk 2 | Sleep onset/quality | Adjust dose/timing |
| Mo 1 | Sleep metrics | Hold/adjust |
| Mo 3 | Durable benefit | Continue vs stop |
| Mo 6 | Sleep status | Taper if stable |

## Safety & compliance gate
- ☐ Investigational consent signed (`consent_investigational_peptide`)
- ☐ Patient education delivered (`patiented_dsip`)
- ☐ Source pharmacy verified
- ☐ OSA screen documented

## Patient education key points
- Not FDA-approved; effects vary person to person.
- We address sleep hygiene and screen for sleep apnea first.
- Inject at bedtime as directed.
- Report daytime grogginess or unusual symptoms.
