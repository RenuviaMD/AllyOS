# Epitalon

## Indication
Longevity and sleep-axis support delivered as short pulsed courses. <!-- NOTE: human data limited/low-quality; telomerase claims unproven -->

## Rule out (contraindications)
- ☐ Active malignancy (precautionary — absolute)
- ☐ Pregnancy
- ☐ Lactation
- ☐ Age <18
- ☐ Hypersensitivity

## Rx
- **Dose:** 5–10 mg SC daily ×10–20 days per pulse
- **Route:** SC
- **Reconstitution:** 10 mg + 1 mL BAC water = 10 mg/mL = 10,000 mcg/mL → 10 mg = 1.0 mL = **100 units** (U-100)
- **Cycle:** 10–20 day pulse, repeat 1–2×/year
- **Stack pairs:** mots-c, ss-31

## Labs (baseline)
- CMP, CBC
- Cancer screen current

## Ongoing care
| When | What to check | What to adjust |
|---|---|---|
| Wk 2 | Tolerance, sleep | Complete pulse |
| Mo 1 | Subjective response | Reassess |
| Mo 3 | Off-cycle; well-being | Plan next pulse vs stop |
| Mo 6 | Cancer ROS | Stop on any red flag |

- Not FDA-approved; longevity claims are unproven.
- Given in short courses, not continuously.
- Tell us any cancer history.
