# Growth Stack (Sermorelin + Ipamorelin)

## Indication
Recovery, body composition, and sleep support in symptomatic adults with low-normal IGF-1. <!-- NOTE: rebuilt — dropped CJC-1295 to avoid redundant dual-GHRH; one GHRH analog + one GHRP is the standard pairing -->

## Rule out (contraindications — union)
- ☐ Active malignancy (absolute)
- ☐ Untreated severe hypothyroidism (treat first)
- ☐ Pregnancy
- ☐ Lactation; age <18
- ☐ Hypersensitivity
- ☐ Non-functional pituitary (won't respond to Sermorelin)

## Rx
- **Dose:** Sermorelin 200–300 mcg + Ipamorelin 200–300 mcg SC nightly
- **Route:** SC, bedtime, empty stomach
- **Reconstitution:** Sermorelin 5 mg + 5 mL BAC = 1 mg/mL → 300 mcg = 0.30 mL = **30u**; Ipamorelin 5 mg + 2.5 mL BAC = 2 mg/mL → 300 mcg = 0.15 mL = **15u** (U-100)
- **Cycle:** 12–16 weeks
- **Components:** sermorelin, ipamorelin

## Labs (baseline)
- **IGF-1, TSH/free T4 ← gating**
- Fasting glucose/A1c, CMP, lipids

## Ongoing care
| When | What to check | What to adjust |
|---|---|---|
| Wk 2 | Tolerance, fluid retention | Reinforce timing |
| Mo 1 | Symptoms, glucose | Hold/adjust |
| Mo 3 | IGF-1, glucose | Titrate to upper-normal IGF-1 |
| Mo 6 | IGF-1, composition | Lowest effective dose |

## Safety & compliance gate
- ☐ Investigational consent signed (`consent_gh_secretagogue`)
- ☐ Patient education delivered (`patiented_growth-stack`)
- ☐ Source pharmacy verified
- ☐ Thyroid treated + cancer screen current ← gating

## Patient education key points
- Not FDA-approved; not synthetic HGH.
- Two peptides, one bedtime injection, empty stomach.
- Report swelling, joint pain, or tingling (too much GH).
- We monitor IGF-1 and blood sugar over time.
