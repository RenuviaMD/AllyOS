# Vitality Stack (PT-141 + Kisspeptin-10)

## Indication
Libido and sexual-function support addressing both central arousal and the hormonal axis. <!-- NOTE: Kisspeptin investigational; PT-141 on-label only for premenopausal HSDD -->

## Rule out (contraindications — union)
- ☐ Uncontrolled hypertension (absolute)
- ☐ Significant cardiovascular disease
- ☐ Active hormone-sensitive malignancy
- ☐ Pregnancy
- ☐ Lactation; age <18
- ☐ Hypersensitivity

## Rx
- **Dose:** PT-141 1.75 mg SC PRN (≤8/mo) / Kisspeptin-10 ~100 mcg SC
- **Route:** SC
- **Reconstitution:** PT-141 2 mg/mL→0.875 mL (compounded); Kisspeptin 2 mg/mL→5u
- **Cycle:** PRN/4–8 weeks; reassess at 8 wk
- **Components:** pt-141, kisspeptin-10

## Labs (baseline)
- **BP controlled documented ← gating**
- LH/FSH, testosterone/estradiol, prolactin; hormone-sensitive cancer screen
- Pregnancy test

## Ongoing care
| When | What to check | What to adjust |
|---|---|---|
| Wk 2 | Nausea, BP, libido | Pre-treat nausea; confirm lead time |
| Mo 1 | Efficacy, hormones, dose count | Keep PT-141 ≤8/mo |
| Mo 3 | Benefit, BP, skin, LH/T | Discontinue if no benefit |
| Mo 6 | Efficacy, BP, hormones | Continue if effective |

## Safety & compliance gate
- ☐ Investigational consent signed (`consent_sexual_health_peptide`)
- ☐ Patient education delivered (`patiented_vitality-stack`)
- ☐ Source pharmacy verified
- ☐ BP controlled + hormone-sensitive cancer screen ← gating

## Patient education key points
- Kisspeptin is experimental; PT-141 is approved only for a specific use.
- Don't use if blood pressure is uncontrolled; report chest pain.
- PT-141 can cause nausea and skin darkening with repeated use.
- Tell us any hormone-sensitive cancer history.
