# Longevity Stack (Epitalon + MOTS-c + SS-31)

## Indication
Healthspan, metabolic, and mitochondrial support. <!-- NOTE: all components investigational/not-approved; thin human data -->

## Rule out (contraindications — union)
- ☐ Active malignancy (all three precautionary — absolute)
- ☐ Pregnancy
- ☐ Lactation
- ☐ Age <18
- ☐ Hypersensitivity

## Rx
- **Dose:** Epitalon 5–10 mg ×10–20 d pulse; MOTS-c 5–10 mg 2–3×/wk; SS-31 5–10 mg daily
- **Route:** SC
- **Reconstitution:** per-component (Epitalon 10 mg/mL→100u; MOTS-c 10 mg/mL→50u; SS-31 10 mg/mL→100u)
- **Cycle:** 6–12 weeks (Epitalon pulsed)
- **Components:** epitalon, mots-c, ss-31

## Labs (baseline)
- **Fasting glucose/insulin (HOMA-IR), A1c ← gating endpoint**
- CMP, CBC, lipids; cancer screen current

## Ongoing care
| When | What to check | What to adjust |
|---|---|---|
| Wk 2 | Tolerance, sites | Continue |
| Mo 1 | Glucose/insulin, energy | Reconsider if no signal |
| Mo 3 | A1c, HOMA-IR, function | Continue only if objective benefit |
| Mo 6 | Metabolic panel | Stop if no durable change |

## Safety & compliance gate
- ☐ Investigational consent signed (`consent_investigational_peptide`)
- ☐ Patient education delivered (`patiented_longevity-stack`)
- ☐ Source pharmacy verified
- ☐ Cancer screen current ← gating

## Patient education key points
- Experimental; longevity claims are unproven.
- We'll measure metabolic markers and stop if they don't improve.
- Tell us any cancer history.
- Report unusual or persistent symptoms.
