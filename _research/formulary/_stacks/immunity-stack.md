# Immunity Stack (Thymosin Alpha-1 + KPV)

## Indication
Immune modulation with anti-inflammatory support in chronic/recurrent infection or inflammatory states. <!-- NOTE: Tα1 non-US approved; KPV preclinical -->

## Rule out (contraindications — union)
- ☐ Transplant on immunosuppression (absolute)
- ☐ Severe active autoimmune disease
- ☐ Pregnancy
- ☐ Lactation; age <18
- ☐ Hypersensitivity

## Rx
- **Dose:** Tα1 1.6 mg SC 2×/wk / KPV 200–500 mcg SC daily
- **Route:** SC
- **Reconstitution:** Tα1 5 mg/mL→32u; KPV 2 mg/mL→25u
- **Cycle:** 8–12 weeks
- **Components:** thymosin-alpha-1, kpv

## Labs (baseline)
- CBC w/ diff, CMP/LFTs, CRP/ESR
- Autoimmune screen; **transplant/immunosuppression status ← gating**

## Ongoing care
| When | What to check | What to adjust |
|---|---|---|
| Wk 2 | Tolerance, autoimmune sx | Continue |
| Mo 1 | CBC/diff, LFTs, CRP | Continue if tolerated |
| Mo 3 | Subsets, markers | Continue per response |
| Mo 6 | Immune status | Lowest effective regimen |

## Safety & compliance gate
- ☐ Investigational consent signed (`consent_immunomodulator`)
- ☐ Patient education delivered (`patiented_immunity-stack`)
- ☐ Source pharmacy verified
- ☐ Transplant/autoimmune status documented ← gating

## Patient education key points
- Tα1 is non-US-approved; KPV is investigational.
- Tell us about transplants, immune-suppressing meds, or autoimmune disease.
- Report new joint pain, rash, or fatigue.
- Generally well tolerated; report site reactions.
