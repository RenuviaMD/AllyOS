# Wolverine GLOW (BPC-157 + TB-500 + GHK-Cu)

## Indication
Aggressive soft-tissue, tendon, or ligament repair that has plateaued on standard rehab.

## Rule out (contraindications — union)
- ☐ Active or prior malignancy (all three angiogenic — absolute)
- ☐ Copper overload / Wilson disease (GHK-Cu)
- ☐ Pregnancy
- ☐ Lactation; age <18
- ☐ Hypersensitivity / copper sensitivity
- ☐ Undiagnosed mass / malignancy red flag

## Rx
- **Dose:** BPC-157 250 mcg + TB-500 2 mg (2×/wk) + GHK-Cu 1–2 mg, SC daily
- **Route:** SC
- **Reconstitution:** per-component (BPC 2.5 mg/mL→10u; TB-500 5 mg/mL→40u; GHK-Cu 10 mg/mL→20u)
- **Cycle:** 6 weeks loading, up to 12 total
- **Components:** bpc-157, tb-500, ghk-cu

## Labs (baseline)
- CBC, CMP, serum copper/ceruloplasmin
- **Age-appropriate cancer screening current ← gating**

## Ongoing care
| When | What to check | What to adjust |
|---|---|---|
| Wk 2 | Tolerance, sites | Continue loading |
| Mo 1 | Functional response, copper | Taper TB-500 to weekly |
| Mo 3 | Outcome ± imaging, copper | Stop if at goal/no benefit |
| Mo 6 | Malignancy ROS | Stop on any red flag |

## Safety & compliance gate
- ☐ Investigational consent signed (`consent_investigational_peptide`)
- ☐ Patient education delivered (`patiented_wolverine-glow`)
- ☐ Source pharmacy verified
- ☐ Cancer screen current + copper baseline ← gating

## Patient education key points
- None of these are FDA-approved; evidence is preclinical.
- Cancer history is an absolute reason not to use this.
- Supports rehab; does not replace PT or surgery.
- Report any new lump, weight loss, or metallic taste.
