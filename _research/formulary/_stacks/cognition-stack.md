# Cognition Stack (Selank + Semax + DSIP + Cerebrolysin)

## Indication
Cognitive performance, stress reduction, and sleep optimization. <!-- NOTE: none FDA-approved in US; Cerebrolysin EU/RU-labeled. Semax/Selank now dosed SC to match individual cards -->

## Rule out (contraindications — union)
- ☐ Pregnancy
- ☐ Lactation; age <18
- ☐ Hypersensitivity
- ☐ Severe renal impairment (Cerebrolysin)
- ☐ Uncontrolled epilepsy / status epilepticus (Cerebrolysin)
- ☐ Untreated OSA (DSIP)

## Rx
- **Dose:** Selank 250–300 mcg SC / Semax 300 mcg SC daily / DSIP 100–300 mcg SC HS / Cerebrolysin 5–10 mL IM ×10–20 d
- **Route:** SC + IM (Cerebrolysin)
- **Reconstitution:** Semax/Selank 5 mg/mL → 300 mcg = 0.06 mL = **6u**; DSIP 2.5 mg/mL→8u; Cerebrolysin pre-mixed ampoule
- **Cycle:** Cerebrolysin 10–20 d course within a 12-wk program
- **Components:** selank, semax, dsip, cerebrolysin

## Labs (baseline)
- CMP (renal), CBC
- Seizure/OSA/psychiatric history

## Ongoing care
| When | What to check | What to adjust |
|---|---|---|
| Wk 2 | Tolerance, cognition/sleep | Continue course |
| Mo 1 | Cognitive/mood/sleep change | Hold/adjust |
| Mo 3 | Off Cerebrolysin; status | Plan next course |
| Mo 6 | Durable benefit | Continue vs stop |

## Safety & compliance gate
- ☐ Investigational consent signed (`consent_investigational_peptide`)
- ☐ Patient education delivered (`patiented_cognition-stack`)
- ☐ Source pharmacy verified (counterfeit risk — Cerebrolysin)
- ☐ Renal function + seizure/OSA history documented

## Patient education key points
- None are FDA-approved in the US; effects are often subtle.
- Cerebrolysin is given as IM/IV courses; report any seizures.
- The peptides are small subcutaneous injections — rotate sites.
- Report agitation, daytime grogginess, or unusual symptoms.
