Where we are

The single-clinic clinical + compliance platform is operational and production-capable — a physician, PT, or staff member can document an entire PI case end to end, generate every required legal and billing document, and run the monthly AHCA medical-director audit. What is built & working

Clinical workflow

  • 12-section visit: check-in → injury → PMH → exam → assessment → plan → orders → discharge
  • Your exact functional exam: spine table + maneuvers, verbal scripts, WNL/Limited/Cannot
  • Visit toggle (Initial/Follow-Up/Final) + Modality toggle (In-Person/Telehealth)
  • Florida facility-originated telehealth: consent capture + auto origination statement
  • ROM → ICD-10 auto-population (sprain+strain+pain, laterality, 7th-char by visit)
  • PT daily + weekly notes with day-by-day timeline from accident date

Legal & billing documents

  • Clinical note with Causation, Prognosis & Physician Certification
  • EMC Certification (FL § 627.736) replicating the clinic's form
  • Imaging order — third-party center or on-site (per-clinic config)
  • Superbill + CMS-1500 (02/12) replica, POS/modifier by modality
  • Needs your data: EIN + fee schedule to print dollar amounts

Integrity & AI safeguards

  • Pre-generation audit gate blocks incomplete / non-compliant notes
  • Same-accident clone guard (≤20% narrative similarity, blocks & logs)
  • No-hallucination rule: documents built only from entered data
  • Signature/encounter integrity checks in the audit

Compliance & security

  • MD Governance: AHCA chart audit, 5–10 charts from rolling 30 days
  • 10 inspection points incl. billing-vs-note match, provider authority
  • Facility registry: licenses, BAAs, equipment, AIRS incidents, contracts
  • Both binder PDFs are 100% admin — zero PHI (initials + chart IDs)
  • Sign-in required; role-based RLS; all anon access removed
